Kabir Dabholkar

I am a PhD student at the Technion, in the group of Omri Barak. I study mathematical models of the brain often employing machine learning, and dynamical systems theory. My research focuses on developing methods to evaluate, compare, and reverse-engineer these models, exploring what they can teach us. Previously, I did my undergraduate studies at IISER Pune, where I got my first exposure to computational neuroscience research in the group of Suhita Nadkarni.
